The Thing Directed by John Carpenter (1982)

Based on Who Goes There?, by John W. Campbell Jr,The Thing takes place in Antarctica, where a group of researchers must try to survive a shape shifting monster that is picking them off 1 by 1

 

 

 

The Shining Directed by Stanley Kubrick (1980)

Based on the novel by Stephen king, The Shining follows a family spending time in a haunted hotel, which slowly drives the husband and father of the family Jack Torrence insane and the remaining people in the hotel must find a way to survive
